In this situation, we have a black bag. Inside this bag, we have 10 balls. 5 of them are black, 3 of them are red and the other 2 are blue ones. The objective of the game is to take, randomnly, 1, 2 or 3 balls from the bag. Then, the page will return the probability of taking out exactly what you took. This was my first project using Javascript statements, even before I learnt how to use functions. Now I have adapted it with advanced Javascript, CSS flexbox and jQuery to print it on the webpage.
